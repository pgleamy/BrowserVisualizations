SoundScape Music Visualizer

Visualize any music playing on your windows pc in high resolution realtime with SoundScape. Try with Deadmau5 playing! Zoom with mouse wheel, orbit with L mouse, pan with R mouse, auto hidden controls menu in top left corner. You must enable the Windows "Stereo Mix" sound device. The visualization tracks 440 frequency channels and reacts at millisecond speed to each channel in an origin spike sphere with zoom/pan/orbit/color controls.

Time Crystal Visualization

Visualization of a Time Crystal. A Time Crystal is a phase of matter in which the physical system exhibits periodic motion without absorbing energy from an external source, breaking time-translation symmetry. Essentially, it's a structure that repeats in time as well as in space. Use the mouse to Pan/Zoom/Orbit, and the top right slider to modify the frequency of the inner helix, whose oscillation controls the oscillation of the other helixes containing it.

Time Crystal Visualizatin
